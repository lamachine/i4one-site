One swarm, many specialists

Each agent has its own expertise and voice. Together they cover the work that usually lives across a dozen disconnected tools.

A team, not a chatbot

Distinct agents for personal assistance, research, coding, and coaching — each with consistent expertise and a recognizable voice, so the right specialist handles the right task.

Google Workspace automation

Triage Gmail, manage your Calendar, and work with Drive, Docs, Sheets, Slides, and Tasks across multiple accounts — each connection scoped to exactly the permissions you grant.

Personal knowledge management

Capture notes, build a living knowledge base, and let the swarm connect ideas over time — with daily notes, automatic linking, and a memory that actually remembers.

Plan-first reasoning

For complex, multi-step work the swarm drafts a plan, predicts outcomes, acts, and reflects — pausing for your approval before it executes anything that matters.

You approve every plan

A human-in-the-loop checkpoint sits in front of every action that changes your data. The swarm proposes; you decide. No silent surprises.

Scoped, least-privilege access

Every account connection uses an explicit, per-account allowlist of permitted actions. The swarm only touches what you've authorized it to.

How it works

Connect, ask, approve. The swarm handles the rest.

01

Connect your accounts

Authorize the Google services you want help with. You choose the scopes; connections are encrypted and revocable at any time.

02

Ask in plain language

Tell the swarm what you need. The right specialist picks it up, drafts a plan, and shows you exactly what it intends to do.

03

Approve & let it run

Review the plan, approve it, and the swarm executes — capturing what it learned back into your knowledge base for next time.
